📖 Chapter 0 · The Backend Origin Story

Before we write a single line of code...
what does backend engineering actually mean?

Imagine this: You’re building a food delivery app. At first, 50 people use it — your simple API works fine. Then, overnight, 50,000 hungry users flood in. Suddenly, your database collapses, requests time out, payments fail, and angry tweets explode. 💥

Most bootcamps teach you how to spin up a CRUD API. But backend engineering is NOT just CRUD. It's about reliability, scalability, fault tolerance, distributed thinking, and maintainable systems that survive real-world chaos.

"Frameworks change every year. But first principles — statelessness, idempotency, CAP theorem, caching strategies — stay forever."

— the 10x engineer's secret

That's why this roadmap exists. You'll learn concepts that transfer across languages (Express, Spring, Go, FastAPI, Rails) and focus on foundations before tools — architecture, trade-offs, and the mental models that separate script kiddies from system architects.
